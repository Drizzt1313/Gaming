The Time Machine

Gaming through the ages

I want to take you back, back to when the dawn of Video games was emerging. Playing sports and other games have always sparked the competitive side in us, but not every person is physically or mentally built for some of these sports. Video games turned the page to a new era and made it possible for anyone with a competitive spirit to throw their hat in the ring and play. Games in the beginning were simple, single pixels, and amounted to nothing but a dot bouncing back and forth, yet it was still a game where people flocked to see who the best was. The evolution of this electric phenomenon has sparked international challenges and even created high-stake televised competitions People didn’t just benefit by boosting their egos though, lots of games were made for us to relax or enter a world that only our imaginations used to be able to take us. They opened new gateways to worlds only thought possible in poorly made movies and illustrated books. Now, we can live inside these worlds and interact with them. This page is meant to show the progression of games in a multitude of ways. Not only showing the growth in technology but also the growth in storytelling, content, and genres, among other things. I want to illustrate how in each decade video games created an inspirational impact on people’s lives. This site will hopefully offer new information that you otherwise would not have known about the video game world, or if you have never interacted with games, show you the positive impact that have made in this world.
